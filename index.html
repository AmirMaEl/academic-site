About

I am a third-year PhD student in the Machine Learning & Perception group at FAU, fortunately supervised by Prof. Vasileios Belagiannis (FAU), Prof. Gustavo Carneiro (University of Surrey, UK), and Prof. Nassir Navab (Technical University of Munich, Germany). My research focuses on bridging sim2real gaps using generative modelling. Prior to the PhD I completed an M.Sc. in Medical Engineering at FAU, collaborating closely with Dalia Rodríguez and Prof. Andreas Maier.
